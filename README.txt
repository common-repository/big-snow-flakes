== Description ==

Winter here? Is Christmas coming? Add nice looking big animated snowflakes to your Wordpress site and enjoy winter and Christmas.

Attract your visitors with these realistic looking nice snowflakes and they will love not just your content but also your seasonal design.

For more information take a look at the [video preview](http://www.wpmaniax.com/seasonal-plugins/)

No configuration needed. Just install and activate. That's all.

== Installation == 

1. Drop the 'big-snow-flakes' folder into your 'wp-content/plugins' folder
 
2. Go to your Administration: Plugins page and activate the "Big Snow Flakes" plugin
